Glass Animals

The English indie rock band was formed in Oxford in 2010. The band consists of four talented members: Dave Bayley (vocals, guitar, keyboards, drums, and songwriting), Drew MacFarlane (guitar, keyboards, and backing vocals), Edmund Irwin-Singer (bass, keyboards, and backing vocals), and Joe Seaward (drums).

Their debut album, Zaba, was released in 2014 and included the hit single "Gooey," which was certified platinum in the U.S. Their second album, How to Be a Human Being, received widespread acclaim and won two categories at the 2018 MPG Awards: UK Album of the Year and Self Producing Artist of the Year. It also earned a spot on the Mercury Prize shortlist.

In 2020, they released their third album, Dreamland, which peaked at number two on the UK Albums Chart and number seven on the US Billboard 200.

The band is best known for their biggest hit single, "Heat Waves," which gained viral popularity on TikTok. The song reached number one in Australia in February 2021 and was voted number one on the Triple J Hottest 100 of 2020. By September 2022, "Heat Waves" surpassed two billion streams on Spotify and reached number one on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100, along with number five on the UK Singles Chart.

At the 2022 Brit Awards, the band received nominations for two Brit Awards: Best British Alternative/Rock Act, and their single "Heat Waves" for Best British Single. Additionally, they received their first Grammy nomination in the Best New Artist category at the 2022 Grammy Awards.

Billie Eilish Biography

Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connell (born December 18, 2001) is an American singer and songwriter. She first gained public attention in 2015 with her debut single, "Ocean Eyes," which was written and produced by her brother, Finneas O'Connell. The two collaborate on music and live performances. In 2017, Eilish released her debut EP, Don't Smile at Me, which achieved commercial success, reaching the top 15 in numerous countries, including the US, UK, Canada, and Australia.

Her debut studio album, When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? (2019), debuted at number one on both the US Billboard 200 and the UK Albums Chart, becoming one of the year's best-selling albums. The single "Bad Guy" marked a significant milestone as it became the first song by an artist born in the 21st century to top the US Billboard Hot 100 and earn diamond certification from the RIAA. In 2020, Eilish performed the theme song "No Time to Die" for the James Bond film, which topped the UK Singles Chart and won the Academy Award for Best Original Song in 2022. Her subsequent singles, including "Everything I Wanted," "My Future," and "Therefore I Am," all peaked within the top ten in both the US and UK.

Eilish's second studio album, Happier Than Ever (2021), achieved number one status in 25 countries. She also wrote and performed "What Was I Made For?" for the fantasy film Barbie (2023), gaining her a second number-one single in the UK and another Academy Award. Her third album, Hit Me Hard and Soft (2024), received critical acclaim, featuring top-five singles like "Lunch" and "Birds of a Feather," with the latter becoming her first number-one on the Billboard Global 200.

Eilish has garnered numerous accolades, including nine Grammy Awards, two American Music Awards, and twenty Guinness World Records. Flume

Harley Edward Streten (born November 5, 1991), known professionally as Flume, is an Australian musician, DJ, and record producer. He is recognized as a pioneer of the future bass genre, helping to popularize it in the music industry. Flume's self-titled debut studio album, Flume, was released in 2012 to critical acclaim, topping the ARIA Albums Chart and achieving double-platinum accreditation in Australia.

Throughout his career, Flume has remixed popular songs from several notable artists, including Lorde, Sam Smith, Arcade Fire, Hermitude, and Disclosure. His second studio album, Skin, was released in 2016 and again topped the ARIA Albums Chart. It won the award for Best Dance/Electronic Album at the 2017 Grammy Awards, and it gained international recognition for its lead single, "Never Be Like You," which was nominated for Best Dance Recording.

Following the success of Skin, Flume released Skin Companion EP 1 in November 2016 and Skin Companion EP 2 in February 2017, both serving as B-side EPs to the album. In 2019, he released a mixtape, Hi This Is Flume, which received positive reviews. His third studio album, Palaces, was unveiled in May 2022. In February 2023, he issued his second mixtape, Things Don't Always Go the Way You Plan. Most recently, his third mixtape, titled Arrived Anxious, Left Bored, was released in May 2023.
