Site Plan for Music Exploration Project

Site Name

The site name is Explore Music Artists. This name emphasizes the site's focus on discovering and showcasing various music artists while being catchy and memorable. It reflects the mission of the website to provide a platform for both artists and fans.
Optional domain availability: exploremusicartists.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a platform for music enthusiasts to explore various artists, their work, and the genres they represent. It will include artist profiles, music recommendations, and a community forum for discussions.

Scenarios

Site Visitor Questions

  • Which artists are trending in the indie music scene?
  • What is the biography of these artists?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: var(--primary-color) (used for headings)
  • Secondary Color: var(--secondary-color) (used for accents and buttons)
  • Accent Color 1: var(--accent1-color) (could be used for highlights)
  • Accent Color 2: var(--accent2-color) (used for background sections)

Typography

Headings: 'Roboto', serif;
Body: 'Roboto', serif;

Wireframe

Mobile View: A single column layout with a logo at the top, followed by featured artists, music recommendations, and a footer with contact information.
Desktop View: A multi-column layout with a navigation bar at the top, a hero section showcasing featured artists, sidebars for recent posts, and a main content area for detailed artist profiles.
